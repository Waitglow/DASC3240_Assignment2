# Introduction

-   [**Please describe the structure of this repository. Who are members? Who is the leader and who contributed the overall structure of this report and how?**]{style="color:darkred;"}\
     YANG, Taoming: In this assignment, I analyzed the ggplot2::msleep dataset, which records sleep duration, body weight, diet, and other traits for 83 mammal species. After data cleaning and missing value treatment, I created four visualizations to address three research questions on sleep patterns. Using plotly, I developed three interactive plots: (1) a histogram titled "Sleep Total Time Distribution of Mammals" to show the overall distribution of sleep duration; (2) a boxplot with jittered points titled "Mammalian Diet vs Sleep Total Time" to compare sleep duration across diet types; and (3) a log-transformed scatter plot titled "Mammalian Body Weight vs Sleep Total Time" to explore the relationship between body size and sleep time. Additionally, using gganimate, I created an animated scatter plot titled "Body Weight vs Total Sleep Time by Diet in Mammals" with smooth transitions to display patterns across different diet categories sequentially. These visualizations enabled effective interactive exploration and clear group comparisons.
